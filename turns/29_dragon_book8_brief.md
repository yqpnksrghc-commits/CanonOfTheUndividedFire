THE ARC FOR BOOK 8 — THE GOOD WORK (Huvarshta, the limb of the Deed):
  The deed closes the circuit. The good mind and the true word become real only when they
  are DONE. And the deed's field is the living world itself — which, in this tradition, is
  GOOD and to be TENDED, not escaped. The good work is concrete: the small patch of
  ground, the person actually shown up for, the thing built or repaired that serves.
