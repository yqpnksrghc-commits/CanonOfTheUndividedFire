     complete (continuity with B3's "does not wait for death").
  6. THE FIRE UNDIVIDED — the culmination of the refrain. B1 multiplied; B2 "multiplied
     yet undivided"; B3 "remains one"; B4: the many flames revealed as ONE Fire, its
     multiplication and its oneness fully reconciled. It was always one; now that
     oneness is manifest.
