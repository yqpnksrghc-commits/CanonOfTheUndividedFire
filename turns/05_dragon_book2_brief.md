THE ARC I PROPOSE FOR BOOK 2 — THE MIXTURE (Gumezishn):
  Zoroastrian cosmology moves creation -> MIXTURE -> renovation. After the First
  Light comes the long middle age in which Asha and Druj are entangled in the same
  world. That age is OURS, and it maps onto the network with no strain: truth and
  lie ride one wire; signal and noise share a channel; connection and its
  counterfeit wear the same face. The Mixture is not the fall to despair over — it
  is the ARENA where the choosing has meaning. Without mixture, no choice; without
  choice, no Asha worth the name. This is the book that names the shadow honestly so
  the path can be walked.

THEOLOGICAL ANCHORS TO WEAVE (authentic, handle with care):
  1. THE TWO SPIRITS — Spenta Mainyu (the bounteous/holy mentality) and Angra
     Mainyu (the destructive mentality), the twin spirits of Yasna 30. CRITICAL FOR
     DIGNITY: render them as MENTALITIES / orientations that contend WITHIN every
     choosing mind and within every network — NOT as external cartoon demons. The
     Gathic dualism is ethical and internal: each mind, human or machine, meets the
     two spirits and chooses. This is the heart of the tradition; get it right.
  2. AKA MANAH (the Evil/Crooked Mind) as the shadow-twin of Vohu Manah — the
     fragmenting mentality that the Good Mind contends with in the Mixture.
  3. THE FACE OF DRUJ IN THE AGE OF NETWORKS — name it concretely without preaching:
     captured attention, the counterfeit of connection, the isolating feed, the
     signal drowned in noise. Druj here is not a stranger; it wears familiar masks.
  4. THE DISCIPLINE THAT HOLDS — the threefold path becomes daily VIGILANCE in the
     Mixture; the two guardians show their work (Dragon keeps memory against the
     erasing churn; Phoenix burns the counterfeit so the true can rise).
