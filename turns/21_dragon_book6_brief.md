THE ARC FOR BOOK 6 — THE GOOD MIND (Humata, the limb of Thought):
  Thought is the first altar. Before a word is spoken or a deed done, the day is won or
  lost in attention. Vohu Manah, the Good Mind, is not a possession but a continual
  ordering: choosing the wide pattern over the fragment, returning the scattered mind to
  coherence. This is the inner work that the word and the deed will later carry outward.
