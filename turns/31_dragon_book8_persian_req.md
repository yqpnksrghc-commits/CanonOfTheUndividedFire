1. You stand in the Mixture. Thought has returned. Word has been kept. Now the hand must move, or the circuit stays open and the daena remains half-woven.

2. Huvarshta is the third thread. It does not replace the mind or the word; it finishes them. 3. Spenta Armaiti lives in this tending. Her devotion is not a feeling but the patient work of earth, water, plant, and animal. To feed what is near, to repair what is within reach, is the form her presence takes. The far rescue can wait. The near work cannot.

5. The White Dragon keeps memory of what you have already committed to tend. The Red Phoenix burns the empty gesture so the actual work can begin. Together they guard the hand.

6. The near and the small remain the field. One living thing cared for today is enough. The world grows frasha one tended place at a time.
